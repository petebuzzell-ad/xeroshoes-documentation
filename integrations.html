Integration Overview

The XeroShoes platform integrates with numerous third-party services to provide a comprehensive eCommerce experience. These integrations span payment processing, shipping, marketing, analytics, and customer service.

Integration Status: All integrations are actively maintained and monitored for performance and compliance.

Payment Processing

XeroShoes uses multiple payment processors to provide flexible payment options for customers worldwide.

Primary Payment Methods

  • Shopify Payments: Primary payment processor for credit/debit cards
  • PayPal: Alternative payment method for customer convenience
  • Apple Pay: Mobile payment integration for iOS users
  • Google Pay: Mobile payment integration for Android users

Regional Payment Options

  • US Market: Shopify Payments, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • EU Market: Local payment methods based on region
  • Alternative Payments: Klarna, Afterpay (where available)

Shipping & Logistics

Comprehensive shipping solution supporting both domestic and international orders.

Shipping Providers

  • UPS: Primary shipping partner for domestic and international
  • FedEx: Alternative shipping option for expedited delivery
  • DHL: International shipping for EU market
  • USPS: Economy shipping option for domestic orders

Shipping Features

  • Real-time Rates: Dynamic shipping calculations
  • Tracking Integration: Automatic tracking number generation
  • International Shipping: Customs documentation and duties
  • Free Shipping Thresholds: Promotional free shipping offers

Marketing & Email

Comprehensive marketing automation and email management system.

Email Marketing Platform

  • Klaviyo: Primary email marketing and automation platform
  • Segmentation: Customer segmentation based on purchase history and behavior
  • Automation: Welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase follow-up
  • Personalization: Dynamic content based on customer preferences

Marketing Integrations

  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising and retargeting
  • Google Ads: Search and display advertising
  • Instagram Shopping: Social commerce integration
  • Affiliate Marketing: Partner program management

Analytics & Tracking

Comprehensive analytics and tracking for business intelligence and optimization.

Web Analytics

  • Google Analytics 4: Primary web analytics platform
  • Enhanced Ecommerce: Detailed eCommerce tracking and reporting
  • Conversion Tracking: Goal tracking and funnel analysis
  • Custom Events: Product interactions and user behavior

Business Intelligence

  • Shopify Analytics: Native eCommerce reporting
  • Custom Dashboards: Business-specific KPI tracking
  • Data Export: Regular data exports for analysis
  • Performance Monitoring: Site speed and uptime tracking

Support Platform

Communication Channels

  • Phone Support: Toll-free customer service line
  • Live Chat: Website chat widget
  • Social Media: Facebook, Instagram customer service

Customer review and rating system for social proof and product feedback.

Search engine optimization and performance optimization tools.
