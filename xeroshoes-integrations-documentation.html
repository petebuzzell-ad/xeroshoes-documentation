Integration Overview

The XeroShoes platform integrates with 36 specialized apps and services to provide comprehensive eCommerce functionality across both US and EU instances. These integrations handle everything from payment processing and inventory management to customer service and compliance.

Integration Status: All 36 apps are actively installed and configured across both XeroShoes.com (US) and XeroShoes.eu (EU) instances, providing seamless functionality for customers and administrators.

Integration Categories

  • Payment & Checkout (6 apps): Payment processing, tax compliance, and checkout optimization
  • Marketing & Email (8 apps): Email marketing, SMS, customer surveys, and loyalty programs
  • Analytics & Tracking (6 apps): Performance monitoring, conversion tracking, and customer insights
  • Customer Service (3 apps): Support tools, reviews, and customer communication
  • Inventory & Shipping (4 apps): Inventory management, shipping, and order tracking
  • Search & Discovery (3 apps): Product search, filtering, and recommendation engines
  • Compliance & Legal (2 apps): GDPR compliance and data protection
  • Utilities & Tools (4 apps): Development tools, redirects, and data management

Troubleshooting

Common integration issues and resolution procedures.

Common Issues

  • Email Delivery Problems:
    • Check Klaviyo account status and billing
    • Verify email authentication settings
    • Review spam folder and delivery logs
  • Payment Processing Errors:
    • Verify payment gateway configuration
    • Check Avalara tax settings
    • Review checkout flow configuration
  • Search Functionality Issues:
    • Rebuild search index in Searchanise
    • Check product data completeness
    • Verify search configuration settings
  • Analytics Data Discrepancies:
    • Verify tracking code installation
    • Check attribution settings
    • Review data sync configurations

Emergency Procedures

  • App Failure: Disable problematic app and contact support
  • Data Sync Issues: Use Matrixify to restore from backup
  • Payment Processing Down: Switch to backup payment method
  • Email System Failure: Use alternative communication channels
