Strategic Platform Overview

The XeroShoes eCommerce platform is a strategic business asset built on Shopify Plus, engineered to drive revenue growth and operational excellence across US and EU markets. Our dual-instance architecture ensures compliance while maximizing market penetration through customized Dawn theme implementation and integrated business intelligence.

Business Outcomes: Platform delivers measurable results through optimized conversion rates, streamlined operations, and data-driven decision making across both regional markets.

System Architecture

The platform architecture is designed for scalability, performance, and ease of maintenance across both regional sites.

Shopify Plus Setup

Both XeroShoes.com and XeroShoes.eu are hosted on Shopify Plus, providing enterprise-grade features including:

  • Advanced checkout customization
  • Multi-store management
  • Enhanced API access
  • Priority support

Dawn Theme Customization

The Dawn theme serves as the foundation with extensive customizations for XeroShoes branding and functionality:

  • Custom color schemes and typography
  • Modified product templates
  • Enhanced navigation components
  • Custom JavaScript modules

Multi-Instance Configuration

Due to legal structure requirements, XeroShoes operates separate Shopify Plus instances rather than using Shopify Markets:

Important: XeroShoes uses separate Shopify instances due to legal and regulatory requirements. This ensures compliance with regional business entity structures and regulations.
  • XeroShoes.com: US market with USD pricing
  • XeroShoes.eu: EU market with EUR pricing

Content Management

Content management is handled through the Shopify Admin interface with custom workflows for different content types.

Product Management

Products are managed through the Shopify Admin with custom fields and automated workflows for inventory management.

Collection Management

Collections are organized by product categories and seasonal campaigns, with automated rules for product assignment.

Page Management

Static pages including About, Contact, and policy pages are managed through the Shopify Admin with custom templates.

Integrations

The platform integrates with various third-party services for enhanced functionality:

  • Payment processing (Shopify Payments, PayPal)
  • Shipping providers (UPS, FedEx, DHL)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics, Shopify Analytics)
  • Email marketing (Klaviyo)
  • Customer service (Zendesk)

Maintenance & Support

Regular maintenance tasks and support procedures are documented to ensure platform stability and performance.
