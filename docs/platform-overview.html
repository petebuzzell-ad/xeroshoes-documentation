Platform Overview

The XeroShoes eCommerce platform is a comprehensive, multi-region solution built on Shopify Plus, designed to support the unique needs of the barefoot and minimalist shoe market. Our platform serves customers across North America and Europe through dedicated regional sites.

Platform Status: Fully operational with 99.9% uptime, serving customers across XeroShoes.com (US) and XeroShoes.eu (EU) markets.

Our platform delivers a seamless shopping experience with features specifically designed for the barefoot shoe market:

Multi-Region Setup

Our platform consists of two separate Shopify instances to comply with legal and regulatory requirements:

Important: Due to legal structure requirements, XeroShoes uses separate Shopify instances rather than Shopify Markets. This ensures compliance with regional regulations and business entity requirements.

XeroShoes.com (US Instance)

  • Platform: Separate Shopify Plus instance
  • Currency: US Dollars (USD)
  • Shipping: UPS, FedEx, USPS with domestic and international options
  • Payment Methods: Credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Content: US-specific product information and marketing
  • Compliance: US tax and shipping regulations
  • Admin Access: Dedicated admin interface for US operations

XeroShoes.eu (EU Instance)

  • Platform: Separate Shopify Plus instance
  • Currency: Euros (EUR)
  • Shipping: DHL, local carriers with EU-wide delivery
  • Payment Methods: Local payment methods and digital wallets
  • Content: EU-specific product information and marketing
  • Compliance: GDPR, EU tax regulations, and consumer protection laws
  • Admin Access: Dedicated admin interface for EU operations

Cross-Instance Management

  • Product Sync: Manual synchronization of product catalogs between instances
  • Inventory Management: Separate inventory tracking for each region
  • Content Updates: Regional content management through respective admin interfaces
  • Analytics: Separate analytics and reporting for each instance
  • Customer Data: Isolated customer databases per region
